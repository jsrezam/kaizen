Sales Tracking

Kaizen helps you improve your sales tracking by campaigns strategy to sell your products.You can create and assign sales campaigns to existing customers in the scope of the application which allows you to record the sales orders achieved by the commercial agents.

Real Work Tracking

With Kaizen you can give the correct follow-up to your sales campaigns since through the follow-up that the system applies to commercials you can be aware of their real performance.

Benefits

Kaizen manages the information that is generated in the execution of sales campaigns, reducing manual work between the supervisor and the agent, providing an informative dashboard that shows key points of the business.
